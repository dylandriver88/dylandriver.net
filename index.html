Aside from my web chops, I am a passionate, team-oriented, and hard-working individual. Critical thinking, organization, and problem solving are my greatest strengths. I enjoy collaborating with my peers in order to create the most effective and compelling designs, but I can also hold my own when it comes to working as a lone wolf.

In my spare time I enjoy cooking, keeping active, golf, video games, and sampling different craft beers. I graduated from Quinnipiac University in 2014 with a B.A. in Interactive Digital Design. Since then, I've worked in multiple roles across multiple industries that brought me from Graphic Designer to Web and Email Designer to Web Developer. I've spent the last decade honing my craft to become the best developer that I can be....

I've been around computers since I was a kid. My father was a software programmer and passed some of his knowledge to me. I also grew up around a lot of art. Many of my family members are good artists, whether it be in music or drawing. I feel as though their love of art transferred to me; as a kid I would always come home from school and draw pictures for hours on end. The mix of art and problem solving that web design and development bring is what makes me so passionate about it.

I specialize in web design & development, but am also well versed in print design, UX/UI design, and I can even code emails!
